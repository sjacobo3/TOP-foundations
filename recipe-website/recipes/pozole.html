Pozole Rojo

Description

Pozole is a traditional Mexican soup made from hominy and meat, typically pork. It's known for its rich flavors and is often garnished with various toppings.

Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 can of hominy
  • 2 lbs of pork, cubed
  • spices:
    • bay leaves
    • oregano
  • chilies:
    • guajillo
    • ancho
    • chile de arbol
  • 6 garlic cloves, halved
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • salt to taste

toppings can include:

  • shredded lettuce
  • thinly sliced radishes (optional)
  • chicaron
  • diced onion
  • queso fresco
  • avocado (optional, but the best part)
  • lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. In a stock pot, bring pork and water to a boil. Make sure to skim off any foam that rises to the top.
  2. Add onion, garlic, bay leaves, and salt to the stock pot. Let simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
  3. With deseed and devein the chilies, boil them in a saucepan with water for 15 minutes.
  4. Blend the chilies with a few cloves of garlic and the chilies cooking water.
  5. Add a little oil to an empty saucepan and strain the blended mixture into the pan. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the chilies misture and hominy to the stockpot and simmer for 30 minutes.
  7. Once served, add toppings. Enjoy!
