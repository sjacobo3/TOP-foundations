Pozole Rojo

A pork-based soup with hominy, and finished with your favorite toppings.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 45 mins

Total Time: 1 hr

Ingredients

  • 1 can on hominy
  • 2 pounds of pork
    • cut into medium sized cubes
  • 6 guajillo chiles
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • salt and pepper to taste

Toppings can include:

  • chopped lettuce
  • chopped radish
  • chopped chicaron
  • chopped onion
  • chopped queso fresco
  • avocado slices
  • lime juice
